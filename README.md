Junior Data Analyst
-------------------

I’m passionate about the world of data analytics and its power to drive meaningful insights and decision-making. My journey into data analytics began during my Bachelor’s degree in Economics, where I was first introduced to R and Python. These tools sparked my curiosity, and I quickly realized the potential of data in solving complex problems and revealing hidden patterns. After graduating, I was determined to continue building my skill set in this field. I deepened my knowledge by learning SQL, which helped me master the art of querying and managing databases. I also explored data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI to bring data to life, making it easier to communicate insights effectively to a wide range of audiences. On this GitHub, you’ll find projects where I apply these skills to real-world datasets, as I continue to explore and grow in the field of data analytics. Let’s connect and discuss data, analytics, and more!
